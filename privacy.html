3. SMS & Text Message Consent

We only send text messages to users who have explicitly checked the SMS consent checkbox on our contact form.

Checking the checkbox means you agree to receive text messages from Methodly about your inquiry, scheduling, and follow-up. Consent is not a condition of purchase or service.

  • Who sends messages: Only Methodly. We do not share your mobile number or SMS opt-in consent with affiliates, partners, third parties, lead buyers, or any unrelated sellers.
  • Message frequency: Varies. You will typically receive messages in response to your inquiry or for scheduling purposes.
  • Message and data rates: Standard carrier rates may apply.
  • To opt out: Reply STOP at any time. We will immediately honor your opt-out and stop all future SMS communications. Your opt-out will be recorded in our CRM as SMS consent = false.
  • For help: Reply HELP. We will respond: "Methodly: You can reach us at automate@methodly.me. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg/data rates may apply."
  • First SMS message: Will include: "Methodly: Thanks for reaching out. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg/data rates may apply."

We do not sell, share, or transfer mobile opt-in consent data or phone numbers to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Email Consent

We only send marketing emails to users who have explicitly checked the email consent checkbox on our contact form. If you did not check that box, we will only send transactional emails directly related to your request.

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every marketing email, or by contacting us at automate@methodly.me.
