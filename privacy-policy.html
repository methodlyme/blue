Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Welcome to Methodly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, business name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • SMS consent status (true/false) and exact consent language at time of submission
  • Email marketing consent status (true/false) and exact consent language at time of submission
  • Timestamp, page URL, IP address, user agent at time of form submission
  • IP address, browser type, device info, usage data collected automatically

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide services
  • To send text messages only if you explicitly opted in via SMS consent checkbox
  • To send marketing emails only if you explicitly opted in via email consent checkbox
  • To send transactional emails related to your request (no opt-in required)
  • To maintain consent records for legal compliance
  • To improve our website and services

3. SMS & Text Message Consent

We only send text messages to users who explicitly checked the SMS consent checkbox. Consent is not required to purchase or use our services.

  • Sender: Only Methodly sends messages. We do not share your number with affiliates, partners, third parties, or lead buyers for marketing.
  • Message frequency: Varies based on your inquiry.
  • Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply.
  • To opt out: Reply STOP at any time. We will immediately stop all SMS and record opt-out in our CRM.
  • For help: Reply HELP. Response: "Methodly: You can reach us at automate@methodly.me. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg/data rates may apply."
  • First SMS: "Methodly: Thanks for reaching out. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg/data rates may apply."

We do not sell, share, or transfer mobile opt-in data to any third party for marketing.

4. Email Consent

We only send marketing emails to users who explicitly checked the email consent checkbox. You may unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting automate@methodly.me.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share with trusted service providers (HubSpot, Twilio, Zapier, Netlify, Cloudflare) who are contractually bound to protect it. We do not share mobile opt-in consent with affiliates, partners, or unrelated sellers.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards. No system is 100% secure. Contact automate@methodly.me with security concerns.

7. Data Retention

Consent records (timestamps, exact language) are retained for a minimum of 4 years for TCPA compliance. Other data is retained as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion at automate@methodly.me.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity and similar tools. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

9. Your Rights

You may access, correct, delete, or opt out of communications. Contact: automate@methodly.me.

10. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.
